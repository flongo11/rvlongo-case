3. Practical and Logistical Challenges

-   Volume of Data: The sheer number of emails sent in a medium-to-large

-   False Positives: Automated systems scanning for keywords (e.g., "new
    job," "resume," "competitor name") are notoriously inaccurate. They
    flag innocent emails, wasting managerial time and potentially
    embarrassing employees.

-   Context is King: An email that looks suspicious to an algorithm
