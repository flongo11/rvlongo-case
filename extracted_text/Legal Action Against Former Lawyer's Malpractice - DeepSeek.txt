--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Narrative: Francesco LONGO is a contractor who was hired Steve and Haley
ZVANIGA in March 2021 to build an in-ground swimming pool in the
ZVANIGA's back yard at 2948 St Clair. It was agreed upon that Steve
would supply the pool and all equipment required. LONGO would install

installation. The pool however is only 70-80% complete. Dispute
